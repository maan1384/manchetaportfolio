About

Graphic Designer | Illustrator

Marvin Ancheta has +3 years of Graphic Design experience, where he has learned to think creatively in order to conceptualize and execute design concepts. He also has skills in web design and has experience in working with traditional materials, such as ink and charcoal.

Before his career in Graphic Design, Marvin studied to become an audio engineer as he had the desire to create music. Aside from Graphic Design, Marvin likes watching anime, likes to create music, and enjoys skateboarding on his free time.
