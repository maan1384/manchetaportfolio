About

Marvin has +3 years of Graphic Design experience, where he has learned to think creatively in order to conceptualize and execute design concepts. He is also skilled in Web Design and has experience in working with traditional materials, such as ink and charcoal.

Before his career in Graphic Design, Marvin studied to become an audio engineer as he had the desire to create music. He pursued this goal by gaining experience from learning how to play several instruments and also working with music mixing software such as Pro Tools. Aside from Graphic Design, Marvin likes watching anime, likes to create music, and enjoys skateboarding on his free time.
