About Me

Marvin Ancheta is currently employed at the Palo Alto Daily Post, where he has learned to think creatively in order to conceptualize and execute design concepts for advertisements real estate, restaurants, and much more. He also has skills in basic web design and has experience in working with traditional materials, such as ink and charcoal.

When he is not designing advertisements, Marvin likes watching anime, playing video games, hanging out with friends and family, plays his guitar, and enjoys skateboarding on his free time.
